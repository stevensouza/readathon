Color Scheme Options for Subdued Aesthetic

Compare eight different approaches to creating a more subdued, professional color scheme

Option A: Neutral Monochrome
Philosophy: Maximum subtlety using grayscale palette with minimal color. Very professional and minimalist.
Best for: Users who want the most subdued, distraction-free interface. Color only appears when absolutely necessary (warnings, success states).
Color Usage: Gray spectrum (white to charcoal), with subtle amber/green for critical status only.

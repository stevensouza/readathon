Design Notes
Why Half-Circle (‚óê)?
  • Visually suggests "partial range" or "subset of days"
  • Single character - very compact and subtle
  • Distinct from other icons already in use
  • Works well in dark background (good contrast)
Design Decisions
  • Icon size: 0.7rem (smaller than metric text)
  • Icon color: Semi-transparent white (subtle)
  • Hover effect: Brightens to full white
  • Tooltip: Bootstrap default with HTML formatting
  • Underline: Dashed, teal color, 2px thickness
